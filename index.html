Brief Overview

As a host of the television series "Skylark Tonight", David Skylark (James Franco) begins to make a name of himself when the director of the show, Aaron Rappaport (Seth Rogen) scores an Interview with North Korea leader and dictator, Kim Jong Un (Randall Park). Shortly after their accoplishment was shown on national television, Agent Lacey (Lizzy Caplan) from the F.B.I. shows up at their front door with a mission for the duo, Assassinate the leader.
