Randall Park (born March 23, 1974) is an American actor, comedian, writer, and director. Park was born and raised in Los Angeles, California to Korean parents. Park is married to Jae Suh Park and they have one daughter. In 2014, Park played a company rep trying to recruit college students in Neighbors, and played Kim Jong-un in another Seth Rogen film, The Interview.
